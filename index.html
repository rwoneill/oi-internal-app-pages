This application is an internal accounting tool used by authorized personnel at Overhead Intelligence Inc. to read and reconcile financial data from the company's own QuickBooks Online environment.

The application is not distributed to the public, not sold, and not made available to any party outside Overhead Intelligence Inc. It is operated solely by company administrators (the CEO and the VP of Operations) on company-managed devices for the purpose of producing internal financial reports, supporting external audits, and maintaining accurate accounting records.
