This page is the disconnect endpoint for the OI Internal QuickBooks Connector.

The OI Internal QuickBooks Connector is an internal-use-only tool. To disconnect or revoke the application’s access to Overhead Intelligence’s QuickBooks Online account, an authorized OI administrator can:

  1. Sign in to QuickBooks Online and navigate to Apps · Connections, then disconnect “OI Internal QuickBooks Connector,” or
  2. Run the internal token-revocation script located in the OI — Accounting controlled-access shared drive, which calls Intuit’s OAuth revocation endpoint (https://developer.api.intuit.com/v2/oauth2/tokens/revoke) and invalidates the stored credentials.

For internal questions, contact roger@overheadintel.com.
