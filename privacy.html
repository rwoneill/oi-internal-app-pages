This Privacy Policy describes how Overhead Intelligence Inc. (“OI,” “we,” or “us”) handles information accessed through the OI Internal QuickBooks Connector (the “Software”).

Scope of this policy

The Software is an internal-use-only tool. It is operated by authorized employees of Overhead Intelligence Inc. (the CEO and the VP of Operations) on company -managed devices for the sole purpose of accessing OI’s own financial records held within OI’s own Intuit QuickBooks Online (“QBO”) account, and for producing internal accounting reports and reclassifications based on those records.

The Software is not distributed, sold, or made available to any party outside Overhead Intelligence Inc. It is not used to access financial data belonging to any other entity. There are no external end users.

What information is processed

The Software accesses, retrieves, and locally stores financial information from OI’s QBO account, including but not limited to:

  • General ledger transactions (date, account, amount, memo, customer/vendor name)
  • Profit-and-loss and balance-sheet report data
  • Chart of accounts and account categorizations
  • Bank-feed transactions and reconciliation data

The Software accesses this information using OAuth 2.0 credentials issued by Intuit to OI’s registered Intuit Developer application. No personal information about end users is collected, because there are no end users other than OI’s own administrators.

How information is stored and secured

Data retrieved by the Software is stored locally on company-managed devices and on Overhead Intelligence’s controlled-access OI — Accounting shared storage. Access to these locations is restricted to authorized OI personnel.

OAuth credentials (refresh tokens, client secrets) are stored in a dedicated restricted-access folder and are never committed to any version-control system, shared with third parties, or transmitted outside OI’s controlled environment.

How information is used

Information accessed by the Software is used solely for OI’s internal accounting, financial reporting, audit support, and reconciliation purposes. No information accessed via the Software is sold, shared with advertisers, used for marketing, or provided to any third party other than as required for OI’s own bookkeeping, audit, regulatory, or legal obligations.

Third-party services

The Software interacts only with Intuit QuickBooks Online through Intuit’s published APIs. The Software does not transmit OI’s financial data to any other third-party service or analytics platform. Intuit’s own privacy practices govern the data while it is held within Intuit’s systems.

Data retention

Financial data retrieved by the Software is retained on OI-controlled storage for as long as OI has a legitimate business or regulatory need to retain it. OAuth credentials are retained only for as long as necessary to operate the Software and are rotated periodically per OI’s internal security practices.

Children

The Software is not directed to and does not knowingly process information about children. It is an internal accounting tool for adult business personnel.

Changes to this policy

OI may update this policy from time to time. The “Effective” date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
